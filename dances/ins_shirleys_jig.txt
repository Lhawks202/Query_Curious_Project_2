====== SHIRLEY'S JIG ======

Dance and Music by Colin Hume.\\
Four couples set\\
Tune: SHIRLEY'S JIG
<code>
Al  Top couple give right hands and cross overs give left to the next person down the line,
    right to the next, left to the last, right to each other and cross pack at the bottom.
A2  Circle eight left half-way (slip step) - do-so-do partner.
B1  Balance and swing partner, finishing on your own side.
B2  Cast from the top to turn the set upside down (no overtaking). Do-si-do left shoulder</code>
