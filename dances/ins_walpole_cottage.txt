====== WALPOLE COTTAGE ======
Sicilian circle, M + 2 W\\
P Shaw p. 24\\
Recording: {{ ::music:walpole_cottage--027.mp3.zip |}}\\
{{ ::music:walpole_cottage-wttd01a-tr18.mp3.zip |}}
   
<code>
   Intro -- each time, lines of 3 fwd and back
   A  Men turn each other Rt (arm)
      Then turn opposite LH lady Lt hand
      each other Rt
      Opposite RH lady Lt
      Then each other Rt
      Own ladies (which one first?  Suggest RH lady)
   B  All W -- RH across, Lt back
      Reels of 3, M give Rt shoulders to Rt H woman
   C  Fwd and Back
      Circle 6 hands half way round (progression)
      forward and back
      Baskets, finishing facing next trio in original 
      direction</code>
