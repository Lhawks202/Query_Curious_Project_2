====== ROAD TO CALIFORNIA ======
(32 bar reel, duple proper contra, Mason's Apron)
   
<code>
   A1 C1 cross down to below C2, face out, join hands on sides and
         balance.  All turn R hand once round and balance again
   A2 with one below turn L hand once round, C1 meet in centre and swing.
      Finish facing down, W on Rt
   B1 C1 down centre, turn as couple, back to cast around C2 who move up
   B2 Rt and LT through.</code>
