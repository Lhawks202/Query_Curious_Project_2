====== Portland Fancy ======
===== or Cracking Chestnuts =====

New England Contra Dance. Published by Elias Howe in 1862  \\
Interpreted by Smukler & Millstone in about 2008. \\
Found in Cracking Chestnuts. \\
 Four Face Four dance
<code>
   4 facing 4
   A       Circle eight once around. 
   B       Right and left through over and back. 
   C1      Ladies chain over and back. 
   C2      Line of 4 forward and back, pass through with opposite to meet a 
           new line of 4.</code>
