====== Dancing with Friends ======


DM improper\\
Tune: Dancing with Friends\\ 
Elizabeth Goosens

<code>
A1   Set to partner, fall back (hands?) Cross and loop into place.
A2   repeat to place.
B1   C1 lead down and around C2 to place,
     M cross L, W cross R
B2   2HT partner 1/2 way, Set to NEXT couple (or to own partner
     at ends)
     Circle L with new couple once around.
</code>
