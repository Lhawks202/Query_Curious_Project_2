====== A Trip to Margate by the Sea ======

Published in Paine in 1807. \\
Albert Simons  1990.  \\
Proper duple minor  longways dance.\\
Tune: A Trip to Margate by the Sea

<code>

 8 ALL set twice to partner Star right 1x
 8 ALL set twice to partner Star Left 1x
 8 Once take hand and slip down center and back
 4 Cast to 2nd place 2s move up
 8 ALL set twice two hand turn your PRTN </code>
