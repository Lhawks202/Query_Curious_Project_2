====== ROXBURGH CASTLE ======
(Long duple, rant, proper)\\
Recording: {{ ::music:roxburgh_castle-b2-052_gmdm.mp3.zip |}}
   
<code>
   A1 C1 and C2 dance round to rt counterclockwise, no hands,
       turn S to RH 4 times (rant step)
   A2 Rt hands across, Lt back (walking)
   B1 C1 lead down middle, dance (rant) back to 2nd place,
      2s moving up
   B2 Ballroom hold, rant or polka once round each other (1s and 2s??)</code>
