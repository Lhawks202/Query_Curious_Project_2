====== Borrowdale Exchange ======

Form:  3 couples in a circle (scatter mixer)

Tune: Borrowdale Exchange
<code>
A1	1-8	All circle L & R.
A2	1-4	Into the middle & back;
	5-8	Do-si-do partner.
B1	1-4	Rh star holding hands with person opposite 
		(and with nobody else);
	5-8	Undo the star - the W whose hand is lowest goes under
		(others arch),
		then the next
		[keep hold of hands].
B2	1-8	Promenade around and make new sets.
</code>
