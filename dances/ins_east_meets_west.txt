====== EAST MEETS WEST ======
 (32 bar jig, duple improper)\\
Tune:  EAST MEETS WEST
<code>
   A1  C1 between C2 lead 4 in line down, turn individually, lead
       back into positions (progression)
   A2  circle left and back
   B1 allemande contrary left, partner Rt, contrary Lt
   B2 half promenade with contrary, half Rt and Lt through</code>
