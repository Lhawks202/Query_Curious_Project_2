====== Tunbridge Beauties ======

Recording: {{ ::music:tunbridge_beauties-13-khb-001.mp3.zip |}}\\
Playford (John Young) in 1710.\\ 
Proper duple minor longways dance.
<code>
Al 1-8 1st man set right ang left to partner and change places with 2nd lady
       1st lady set to partner and change places with 2nd man
A2 1-8 1st couple set right and left and cross over; then 2nd couple the same
Bl 1-8 1st and 2nd men lead between the ladies and cast back to place; all
       clap and circle left halfway
B2 1-8 Ladies lead between the men, all clap and circle left halfway.  

</code>
