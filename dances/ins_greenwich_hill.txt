====== Greenwich Hill ======
 Francis Werner 1785\\
Longways for three couples\\
Tune: Greenwich Hill
<code>
Al 1-8 All set right and left and cross over. 
       set right and left and cross back
A2 1-8 1st and 2nd couples right hand star and left hand star back
B1 1-4 1st couple lead down the middle turn individually,
B2 1-4 Dance back and cast to the middle, 2nd couple moving up
Cl 1-4 Circle hands six to the left
C2 1-4 circle hands six to the right and 1st couple cast to the bottom</code>
