====== New Princess, The ======
  

Form: Longways duple improper\\	
Music: 32 bar Jig "New Princess", "Early Riser" by Chris Dewhurst\\
Author: Lena Rees. Still Not Quite Playford, 1988\\
Recording: {{ ::music:new_princess--early_riser-snqp-a8.mp3.zip |}}

<code>
A1	Right then left hand star

A2	Facing up and down the set, full ladies chain

B1	Dance a double figure of eight, 
        started by 1Co casting 
        while 2Co cross over leading up.

B2	Neighbour swing, ending in progressed places.  
        Partner two hand turn
        (Top 2 1 imp)
</code>
