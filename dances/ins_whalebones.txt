======  Whalebones ======
Scottish Country Dance \\ 
Tune: Whalebones\\

4 couple set dance
<code>   
   A1 1-4 C1 make arch, C2 through, cast down and
      5-8 RH star with 3s (in middle)
   A2 1-4 C4 make arch, C3 through, cast up and
      5-8 LH star with 2s (in middle)
   B1 1-8 C2, C3, C4 arches while C1 cast to bottom 
          and back up through the arches (whalebones)
   B2 1-8 C1 lead single cast and arch at bottom,
          others lead through to progressed place.</code>
