====== NERVOUS BREAKDOWN ======
(Bernard Clark, circle mixer)
(any 32 bar reel 2/4 or C/, couples standing in circle, all face in)

<code>
   
   A1 RH turn partner all way and a bit more and LH turn the next
      (W clockwise, M counterclockwise)
   A2 Cross hand turn next
      do-si-do the next
   B1 swing the next (8 bars) finishing with W on Rt (outside)
      of circle)
   B2 Promenade with cross hands (8 bars), reform to start again</code>
