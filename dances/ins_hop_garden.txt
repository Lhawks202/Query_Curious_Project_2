====== The Hop Garden ======
 Unidentified source\\
Albert Simons 1990?\\
Longways Duple Minor\\
Tune: Hop Garden\\
<code>
Al 1-8 All set right and left twice and right and left through (2 charges)
A2 1-8 Repeat to place
Bl 1-8 1st couple lead down the middle, turn individually, dance back
       and cast below the 2nd couple, 2nd couple moving up
B2 1-8 1st and 2nd couples right hand star and left hand star back</code>
