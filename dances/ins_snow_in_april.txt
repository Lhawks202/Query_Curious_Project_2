======  Snow in April  ======

Cyril Jones (2008) to note a rare snowfall in England in April\\
Three couple set. Waltz time.
<code>
Part I

   Set to the right and back
   Cross with partner right shoulder
   Set to the left and back
   Cross with partner left shoulder

Chorus:

   Circle 6 left and right
   Top couple waltz to the bottom as other couples face up, step up,
   All turn two hands to new places.

Part II

   Set to the right and back
   Side R shoulder with partner
   Set to the left and back
   Side L shoulder with partner

Chorus --

Part III

As Part II but with arming. 

Chorus.
</code>
