====== NOTTINGHAM SWING ======

Fried de Metz Herman's  1995.\\ 
Proper duple minor longways dance.\\
16 bar jig, long duple\\
Recording: {{ ::music:nottingham_swing--005.mp3.zip |}}\\
{{ ::music:nottingham_swing-track16.cdda.mp3.zip |}}
   
<code>
   A1 4 M1 & W2 Rt elbow turn with step hop
   A2   M2 & W1 same
   B1   C1 down middle (2 chassis or 2 steps), turn in, up and cast to 
        2nd place, 2s moving up. 
        C1 swing with step hop
</code>

Video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M9imXg0D4CM\\
Nottingham Swing is a dance that Fried says started every dance evening in Nottingham when she lived there
