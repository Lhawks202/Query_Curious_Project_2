====== We Won't Go Home Until Morning ======

England in ~1909.\\
Cecil Sharp  published it in "The Country Dance Book" (Part 1) in 1909.  \\
Proper duple minor longways dance.\\
Tune: We Won't Go Home Until Morning

<code>
A  1-4	Right hands across...
   5-8	... and left hands back
B1 1-4	All clap on 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 5th, 6th, 7th counts
A2 4	All clap
A3 1-8	All partner swing</code>
