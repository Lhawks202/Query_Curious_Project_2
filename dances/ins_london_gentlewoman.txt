===== The London Gentlewoman =====

or The Hemp Dresser

<code>
   Lead up a double forward and back. That again.
   First man change with the second woman. Then with the third man. Then with the fourth woman And so to the last.
   Side all. Lead up as before.
   As before the doing it. All do this, changing as before.
   Side all. That again.
   First couple cross over, fall into the second place. 
   Cross again, fall into the third place. And so to the end, every couple doing the like.
   Arm all. That again.
   Turn your own woman changing places. Turn each the second. 
   Turn each the third And so to the last rest following.</code>


Recording: Belshazzar's Feast, John Playford's Secret Ball
