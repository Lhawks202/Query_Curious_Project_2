====== WALKAWAY ======
Dance: Pat Skelton. \\
Music: Colin Hume.\\
Longways duple improper.\\
Tune: WALKAWAY
<code>
Al  Do-si-do neighbor. Swing neighbor, finishing with the first man and second lady
    facing up, second man and first lady facing down (men in the middle).

A2  Promenade in this direction (3 bars). Turn as a couple and come back (3 bars) 
    Half a ladies chain, but only turn three quarters, so that ones face up, twos down.

Bl  Promenade (3 bars). Turn and come back (3 bars). Half a ladies chain (2 bars)

82: Half promenade. Ladies cross over; men cross over. </code>
