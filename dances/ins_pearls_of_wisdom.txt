====== Pearls of Wisdom ====== 
4C longways in waltz time
   
<code>
   on r diagonal set and cross R shoulders
   on l diagonal same
   Ends meet, cast round to stand behind middle couples while
   middle set and pass through R shoulder to face end couples
   (set is now across the hall)
   L shoulder half hey, finish opposite partner
   B to B with P and 2H turn
   All 8 circle 3/4 to finish longways and cross R shoulder</code>
