====== The Sprigs of Laurel ======

Playford's Dancing Master of 1710.\\ 
Interpreted by Bernard Bentley in 1977. \\
Proper duple minor \\
Tune: Sprigs of Laurel

<code>
A   Set twice to partner
    Star right all the way around
    set twice to partner
    Star Left all the way around
B   Once lead down turn lead back and cast
    down as twos lead up
    Partners right hand turn
    and left hands back</code>
