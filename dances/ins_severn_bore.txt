====== Severn Bore ======

Fried de Metz Herman  1999. \\
Proper duple minor dance.\\
Tune: Severn Bore
<code>
A1 1-2 first man set R forward to 2nd women and L to PRN 
   3-4 turn single home to place
   5-8 Ones and second woman circle 1x
A2 1-8 first woman do the same 
        one's and second man circle
B1 1-8 one's lead down as two's cast up and lead down 
       one's turn all go up     
       twos split the ones in mirror gypsy neighbor 3/4
B2 1-6 Face same sex NBR for hey for four 
   7-8 1st cpl left hand turn into progressed place</code>
