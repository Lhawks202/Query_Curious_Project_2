====== LOXLEY FIGURE EIGHT ======
(3 couples longways)\\
Recording: {{ ::music:loxley_figure_eight--016.mp3.zip |}}
   
<code>
   A1  C1 cross over, heys for 3 on sides
   A2  C1 cross back, heys on own side
       Finish C1 facing down, C2 and C3 facing up
       all nearer hands joined
   B1  C1 and C2 set and C1 pass under C2 arch
       C1 and C3 repeat
   B2  Partners swing    
</code>
