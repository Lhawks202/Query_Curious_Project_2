Miss McLeod's Reel
<code>
   Traditional, usually danced to the tune of the same name
   Duple and proper
         A1 Actives down the center (4 beats); Turn as a couple (4)
         Come back and cast off (8)
         A2 Ladies chain across (8)
         Ladies chain back (8)
         B1 Promenade across (8)
         Right and left back (8)
         B2 Long lines, foward and back (8)
         Actives cross (and bow) (8)</code>
