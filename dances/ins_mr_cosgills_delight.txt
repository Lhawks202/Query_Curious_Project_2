====== MR COSGILL'S DELIGHT ======
(duple longways  Walsh 1728  2   steps/measure)
   
<code>
   A1 1-4  C1 and C2 Rt hands across
   A2 1-4            Lt
   B1 1    1st corners change places
      2    2nd corners change places
      3-4  all circle clockwise (to lt 1/2 way to original places)
           (original says no hands)
   B2 1-4  face partners and 3 changes of circular hey)</code>
