====== Waltz Country Dance ======

Sicilian circle
<code>   
   A1  Take right hands with opposite, balance forward and back, 
       forward again and change places with opposite turning the woman under your joined hands. (1-4)
       Repeat with partners. (5-8) 
   A2  Repeat A1. 
   B1 & B2
       Balance the circle in and out, then roll the women from left to right (1-2)
       Repeat movement 3 times (4 times in all).
       (Women move around the circle, but the men stay in place) 
   B3  Waltz on to next couple.</code>
