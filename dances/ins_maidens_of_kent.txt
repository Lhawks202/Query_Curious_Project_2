====== The Maidens of Kent ======
 Thompson 1794\\
Longways for three couples\\
Tune: The Maidens of Kent
<code>
Al 1-8 1st couple set right and left twice to the 2nd lady and circle
       left with her
A2 1-8 Repeat with the 2nd man
Bl 1-8 1st couple lead down the middle, turn individually, dance back
       and cast to the middle, 2nd Couple moving up
B2 1-8 Circle hands six and 1st couple cast to bottom,
       3rd couple moving up.</code>
