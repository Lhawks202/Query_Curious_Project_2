====== 94 SOUTH STREET ======
By Bill Cochran\\
Duple Minor Proper

<code>
   1. Actives down middle, cast off and come back 
   2. Gypsy partner clockwise in middle, swing opposite
   3. 1's 1/2 eight round 2's, 1's swing in middle
   4. 2 men turn by L. & then R. 
        whilst 2 ladies turn by R. & then L.</code>

music-\\
Named in fond memories of where Tod Whittemore used to live.
