====== Number 11 ======
By Mike Richardson
Improper contra
<code>
  
A1   Circle four half way, swing neighbour on far side 
A2   End the swing facing down, lead down in a line of four, turn as couples
     come back, bend the line 
B1   Circle left three quarters, swing partner 
B2   Long lines forward and back
     Circle left three quarters, California twirl partner into progressed places</code>
